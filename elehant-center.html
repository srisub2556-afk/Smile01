About Lampang Province

Lampang is a province with a unique identity, located in northern Thailand. It has a long history and preserves Lanna culture beautifully.

Lampang is the only city in Thailand that still uses horse carriages as a means of transportation and is also an important center for Thai elephant conservation.

Wat Phra That Lampang Luang

Wat Phra That Lampang Luang is an important historical and cultural temple featuring beautiful Lanna architecture and a sacred stupa, which is a symbol of Lampang Province.
